Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance for weddings and events. However, we accept rush bookings depending on our current availability. Contact us to check!

What is your cancellation policy?

A non-refundable deposit of 30% is required to hold your date. The remaining balance is due 7 days before your event. Please contact us for cancellation details.

How long to receive edited photos?

edited photos are typically delivered within 2-3 weeks. We offer express delivery options for an additional fee if you need them sooner.

Do you offer unlimited revisions?

Yes! We offer unlimited revisions on all our photo editing packages until you're completely satisfied. Your satisfaction is our top priority.

Do you provide travel services?

Yes, we provide photography services locally and nationally. Travel fees may apply for destinations outside Bilaspur. Contact us for specific quotes and details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and digital payment methods like UPI. A deposit is required to confirm your booking.
